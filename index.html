About Me:

My name is Julian Benchimol. I am 23 years old and live in Norwich, CT. I graduated with a degree in game design, and have transitioned into web design. I am looking forward to see where this career path takes me! When I'm not designing, I love to play video games that have rich and engaging stories. I also love to play with my corgi Peach, who is almost one year old!
